Valdemar Legacy

Located in the Rioja region of Spain, Conde Valdemar, one of the most iconic wine collections from the Valdemar family, was founded in 1889 in Oyón. They continued to create wine until in the 1980s, Jesús Martínez Bujanda (4th generation) and his father founded Bodegas Valdemar. They became pioneers, creating the first barrel-fermented white wine and introducing varieties like Maturana and Tempranillo Blanco. Located in Rioja, Spain, the family has spent over 130 years crafting wines from their own vineyards, maintaining a tradition of quality and craftsmanship.

Rebranding 2027

For the upcoming 10th anniversary of Conde Valdemar, we are excited to introduce the rebranding of our renowned wine collection, including Rosé and Blanco, to international markets. This milestone offers the perfect opportunity to refresh our visual identity, with an emphasis on refined typography and contemporary design that enhances both clarity and elegance. Our new labels are crafted to connect with both modern and traditional wine lovers, ensuring that the essence of Valdemar resonates with a diverse audience. This rebranding not only honors our legacy but also embraces the future, reflecting not just a new look, but a more sophisticated and impactful design that elevates the character and craftsmanship that define every bottle of Valdemar wine."

Conde Valdemar Rose

Year: 2027

Region: Rioja, Spain

Flavor Profile: The wine has a soft pink color and delicate aromas of rose petals, wild strawberries, and raspberries, with a hint of white pepper. On the palate, it’s smooth and silky, offering a fresh, well-balanced, and sweet finish. Elegant and vibrant to enjoy!

Temperature: 6/8ºC

Vol.: 12.5%

Blend: 80% Garnacha & 20% Viura

Production: Direct bleeding (saignée) at low temperature, fermented in stainless steel tanks at 16ºC for 20 days.

Pairing Suggestions

Smoked Fish Soups Cream Salads Mediterranean Cousine

Conde Valdemar Blanco

Year: 2027

Region: Rioja, Spain

Flavor Profile:This wine has a pale yellow color with greenish hues and fresh aromas of pear, citrus, herbs, and white flowers. On the palate, it’s smooth and flavorful with a long finish, combining fruity and floral notes for a refreshing and well-balanced experience.

Temperature: 6/8ºC

Vol.: 12%

Blend: 77% Viura, 10% Malvasía, 7% Sauvignon Blanc & 6% Garnacha Blanca

Production: Fermented in stainless steel tanks at 16ºC for 15 days, followed by ageing on fine lees.

Pairing Suggestions

Grilled Fish Salads Vegetables Pasta Dishes Shellfish Smoked Food Creamy Dishes
