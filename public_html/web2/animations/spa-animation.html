MY MUSE

ABOUT

Welcome to my SPA animation project! I’m excited to share a little bit about my love for music, which has always been a huge part of my life. It’s shaped the person I am today. In this space, I’ll be sharing a bit about my personal journey with music, the instruments I play, and some covers I’ve made in my free time. I’ll also introduce you to a few of my favorite artists who I love to listen to and songs to cover! Whether you're a music lover or just curious about my passion, I hope you find something that connects with you here. Thanks so much for visiting!

MY STORY

Ever since I was little, I've loved music. When I was 3 years old, I held a toy microphone and sang with all my heart on top of a coffee table in front of my family. Little did I know that I would come to love it, and I did as I grew older. I would make covers with her at her place or even teach myself new instruments to play. I even started making my own covers and discovering what kind of music genres I loved to listen to and sing!

INSTRUMENTS

Here are the instruments I play. Click on them for more details!

UKELELE

The ukulele was the first instrument I ever played. I got it as a birthday gift, and over time, I taught myself how to play chords and then songs.

PIANO

Through a few piano lessons and with the help of my cousin, I learned how to play the piano. I'm not the best, but I can play easy songs!

GUITAR

Like the ukulele, I taught myself how to play the guitar. I first learned by playing my dad's guitar. To this day, I mainly play the guitar, covering songs either on my own or with my cousin!

CLARINETTE

I first learned how to play it in high school during my music program but it's been a while so I'm a bit rusty.
